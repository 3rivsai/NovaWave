Have a Question? See Our FAQs

Question: Where should I set up my Novawave antenna?

Novawave antenna gets the best reception when placed against a window, but it will work almost anywhere. We suggest experimenting with different locations to see what gives you the most channels.

Question: Can I install my Novawave antenna outside?

No, Novawave antenna is not weatherproof, it was designed for indoor use only. Placing it outside will not improve reception.

Question: Do I need an internet connection for Novawave antenna to work?

No, all you need is a TV!

Question: How many channels will I be able to access?

The channels available will depend on your distance from the nearest broadcasting tower and other environmental factors. Most people find they get dozens of channels, or even hundreds of channels.

Question: Will one Novawave antenna give me free channels on every TV in my home?

No, each TV in your home will require its own antenna to access free channels.

Question: If I move or adjust the Novawave antenna’s location, do I need to rescan for channels?

Yes, any time you move the Novawave antenna, you probably will need to rescan.
